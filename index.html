Martin Tejeda

Product Designer at Underbelly.

Greetings everyone! My name is Martin, sushi lover from San Diego, California. I’ve dove into many career paths before finding my way into Product Design; including nutrition education, physical therapy, and business consulting. My introduction to the UX world came in 2016 when I co-founded a mobile business resource center in Los Angeles. I didn’t know where to start so I Googled everything I could about crafting beautiful user experiences. What excites me the most about working with the Underbelly team is the diversity and autonomy we have on our projects. We can make a huge impact on a company while still not giving up who we are as individuals, and that speaks volumes about the culture we have here. I also love that we have such an active pulse on the Salt Lake City community, and actively work to create events and bring people together. When I’m not designing, I like to stay pretty active outdoors! My go-to activities are Brazilian jiu-jitsu and trail running. Another interesting (and useless) fact about me: I can recite the alphabet backwards in under 5 seconds.
